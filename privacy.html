Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2026

Mezzanine Ledger (“we,” “us,” “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, contact us, or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information - such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Client-provided information - information you choose to share for bookkeeping, tax preparation, or support services.
  • Website usage information - such as pages visited, device/browser information, and general analytics data (if enabled).

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Communicate with you about quotes, onboarding, scheduling, and service updates.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, and prevent fraud or misuse.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to:

  • Service providers that help us operate (such as email, cloud storage, scheduling, payment processing, CRM, or messaging providers).
  • Professional or legal compliance needs (for example, responding to lawful requests or protecting rights and safety).
  • Coordination with your authorized representatives (for example, your CPA/EA) when you request or approve it.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide services, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Your Choices

You may request to:

  • Access or update your contact information.
  • Ask questions about how your information is used.
  • Opt out of certain communications (see SMS section below).

7. SMS/Text Messaging Privacy

If you provide your phone number and opt in to receive text messages from Mezzanine Ledger, we may send messages related to appointment scheduling and reminders, document requests, status updates, and client support.

Consent: You opt in by providing your number and agreeing to receive messages (for example, via a web form checkbox, intake form, or written confirmation). Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Opt-out: You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. You may receive a final confirmation text. You can request help by replying HELP or contacting us at sean@mezzledger.com.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies.

Fees: Message and data rates may apply.

Carriers: Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

SMS Data Sharing: We may use a messaging provider to deliver texts. We share only the information necessary to send messages (such as your phone number and message content).

No sale of SMS data: We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number for third-party marketing.

Mobile information: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

8. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new Effective Date.
